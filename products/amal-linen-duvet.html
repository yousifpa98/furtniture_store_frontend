Story

Near Lake Como sits a prolific textile mill, where expert artisans continue a legacy of over 100 years of passion and quality. Blending time-honored techniques and new weaving methods, they create a luxurious assortment of textured bedding pieces offering a lived-in feel with supreme softness. Our Amal Bedding Collection features linen fabric's signature durability and casual appeal with a bold digital-printed floral pattern, ensuring high-quality comfort with striking style in any bedroom suite.
